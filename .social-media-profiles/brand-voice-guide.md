## 🧩 The Three Pillars — Summary for All Platforms

### 🎓 Mwenaro Academy
**What it is:** An AI-powered, project-based Learning Management System (LMS)
**What it does:** Trains aspiring developers through structured programs from Web Foundations to Fullstack Engineering, with real-world projects, mentor guidance, and AI-assisted code review.
**Key messages:**
- "Project-based programs that produce job-ready developers"
- "AI-powered feedback before instructor review"
- "Verified certificates and real portfolios — not just theory"
- "Self-paced or mentor-guided pathways"
**URL:** https://academy.mwenaro.tech

### 💼 Mwenaro Talent
**What it is:** Africa's curated digital talent marketplace
**What it does:** Connects vetted, trained tech professionals (engineers, frontend architects, product designers) with global employers and opportunities.
**Key messages:**
- "Every candidate completed real production-level projects"
- "Structured technical assessments, portfolio review, Git workflow proficiency"
- "Developers ready to contribute from day one"
**URL:** https://talent.mwenaro.tech

### 🔬 Mwenaro Labs
**What it is:** The R&D and custom software delivery arm
**What it does:** Designs, builds, and maintains scalable web platforms, AI automation systems, and digital transformation solutions for startups and enterprises.
**Key messages:**
- "Custom web and software platforms for reliability and scale"
- "AI & automation for smarter decision making"
- "We partner with founders and enterprises to ship digital products that matter"
- Products built: Mwenaro Flow (workforce matching engine), SafariLink CRM (East African logistics)
**URL:** https://labs.mwenaro.tech

## 🎙️ Brand Voice

| Tone Attribute  | Description                                                                  |
|-----------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Confident**   | We know our value. We speak with authority about African tech potential.      |
| **Inspiring**   | We celebrate wins, uplift learners, and highlight African excellence.         |
| **Clear**       | No jargon for jargon's sake. We explain things plainly and practically.       |
| **Professional**| We are premium. No typos, inconsistent casing, or low-effort posts.          |
| **Warm**        | We are community-driven. We speak to people, not at them.                    |

**DO say:**
- "Africa's tech ecosystem"
- "Structured, project-based learning"
- "Job-ready developers"
- "Built in Africa. Built for Scale."
- "Learn → Showcase → Innovate"

**DON'T say:**
- "cheap courses" / "easy way to learn" (we are premium)
- Generic startup clichés with no substance
- Overly formal corporate language that feels cold
