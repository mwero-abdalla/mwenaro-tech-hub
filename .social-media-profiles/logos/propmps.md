# Canva Logo Design Prompts for the Mwenaro Ecosystem

This document provides both **Canva Search/Design Instructions** (for manually assembling your logos using Canva's elements and fonts) and **Magic Media Prompts** (if you want Canva’s AI Text-to-Image tool to generate inspiration or base assets). 

All 4 logos should feel like they belong to the same family, using the Mwenaro brand identity.

---

## 🎨 Global Brand Settings (Apply to all 4 logos)
- **Primary Color**: Warm orange-red (`#E85D3B`)
- **Backgrounds**: Dark charcoal/near-black (`#09090B`) for dark mode, or pure white for light mode.
- **Typography / Fonts to use in Canva**: `Inter`, `Montserrat`, `Plus Jakarta Sans`, or `Space Grotesk`. 
- **Vibe**: Premium, "Elite Tech Africa", clean, minimalist, high-impact.

---

## 1. Mwenaro Hub (The Main Tech Hub)
**Concept**: The central nexus that connects everything (Academy, Talent, Labs). It represents your foundation, community, and the overarching mission of building African tech.

### 🛠️ Canva Manual Design Instructions:
- **Elements to Search**: "Tech Hub", "Abstract M", "Network Nodes", "Digital Connections", "Geometric M"
- **Layout**: A strong, abstract geometric "M" that subtlely hints at connectivity or upward momentum. 
- **Font pairing**: "Mwenaro" in Bold, "Hub" or "Tech" in Regular/Light weight underneath.

### 🤖 Canva AI Prompt (Magic Media):
> "A minimalist, modern tech startup logo featuring an abstract letter 'M'. The design should incorporate subtle themes of digital connection, central hubs, and networks. Use a solid warm orange-red color on a sleek dark charcoal background. Flat vector style, clean lines, premium tech aesthetic, geometric."

---

## 2. Mwenaro Academy (The LMS / Learning Arm)
**Concept**: Growth, learning, structure, and moving from foundation to mastery. It should signify an elite, project-based education.

### 🛠️ Canva Manual Design Instructions:
- **Elements to Search**: "Code brackets", "Digital spark", "Tech graduation", "Upward arrow geometry", "Tech book abstract"
- **Layout**: Combine coding symbols (like `</>`) with a subtle upward growth or spark motif. Keep the lines stroke-weight consistent with the Mwenaro Hub logo.
- **Font pairing**: "Mwenaro" (Bold) + "Academy" (tracking/letter-spacing increased slightly for a premium, academic feel).

### 🤖 Canva AI Prompt (Magic Media):
> "A sleek, modern app logo for an elite tech academy. The icon should creatively combine coding symbols like brackets with an abstract upward arrow or spark of knowledge. Warm orange-red and white colors on a dark charcoal background. Minimalist, flat vector art, professional tech education brand, simple and memorable."

---

## 3. Mwenaro Talent (The Digital Marketplace)
**Concept**: Connecting top-tier, vetted African talent with global opportunities. Focuses on people, quality, verification, and precision matching.

### 🛠️ Canva Manual Design Instructions:
- **Elements to Search**: "Verified badge", "Tech handshake", "Abstract human structure", "Target digital", "Star quality"
- **Layout**: A stylized star, a sharp checkmark integrated with tech nodes, or a geometric representation of two pieces fitting perfectly together (like a lock/key or puzzle piece).
- **Font pairing**: "Mwenaro" (Bold) + "Talent" (Medium or SemiBold).

### 🤖 Canva AI Prompt (Magic Media):
> "A premium digital talent marketplace logo. Minimalist vector art featuring a concept of professional connection, a vetted tech badge, or elite human capital. Uses a warm orange-red palette against a dark near-black background. Clean, geometric, abstract, representing top-tier software engineers and global hiring."

---

## 4. Mwenaro Labs (The R&D and Software Delivery Arm)
**Concept**: Innovation, scalable engineering, solid software architecture, and the future.

### 🛠️ Canva Manual Design Instructions:
- **Elements to Search**: "Hexagon tech", "Digital building blocks", "Isometric cubes", "Microchip nodes", "Architecture grid"
- **Layout**: A sharp, structured geometric shape (like an isometric cube or connected hexagon nodes) showing scale and strength. 
- **Font pairing**: "Mwenaro" (Bold) + "Labs" (Use a slightly technical font for 'Labs' like `Space Mono` or `Fira Code` in Canva to give it an engineering R&D feel).

### 🤖 Canva AI Prompt (Magic Media):
> "A futuristic, minimalist logo for a custom software R&D lab. The design should feature geometric shapes, abstract code nodes, or digital building blocks representing scale and solid architecture. Warm orange-red and dark zinc background. Sleek, scalable, tech-forward, flat vector graphic, no text in the main image."
