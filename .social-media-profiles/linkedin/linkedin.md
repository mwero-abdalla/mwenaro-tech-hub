## 📝 About Section (2,000-character limit)

> **Mwenaro** is Africa's premier tech ecosystem — a structured, end-to-end platform connecting learning, talent, and innovation to accelerate digital growth across the continent.

We operate across three interconnected pillars:

**🎓 Mwenaro Academy** — A premium, AI-powered learning management platform. We offer structured, project-based programs that produce job-ready developers. From web fundamentals to fullstack engineering, every program is built around real production-level projects, expert mentorship, and AI-assisted feedback on code. Learners earn verified certificates and build real portfolios — not just theoretical knowledge.

**💼 Mwenaro Talent** — Africa's curated digital talent marketplace. We spotlight vetted tech professionals — engineers, frontend architects, and product designers — trained through our structured ecosystem. Employers get access to developers who are ready to contribute from day one, with proven portfolios and structured technical assessments.

**🔬 Mwenaro Labs** — Our R&D and custom software division. We design, build, and maintain scalable web platforms, AI-driven systems, and business automation tools for startups and growing organizations. From custom CRM systems to AI/automation pipelines, we deliver reliable digital solutions with real-world impact.

**The Mwenaro Lifecycle:**
Learn → Showcase → Innovate

With 100+ active learners, 10+ expert-led courses, and reach across 3+ countries, Mwenaro is building the next generation of African tech talent — and the digital infrastructure to support them.

> - Talent: https://talent.mwenaro.tech
