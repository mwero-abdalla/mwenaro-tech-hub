## 📝 Long Description / About (full)

> **Mwenaro** is Africa's structured tech ecosystem — where learning, talent, and innovation come together to shape the next generation of African tech professionals.

### 🎓 Mwenaro Academy
Our AI-powered learning platform offers structured, project-based programs for aspiring developers. Whether you're a complete beginner or leveling up your skills, our curriculum — covering Web Foundations, React Frontend, Backend APIs, and Fullstack Engineering — is designed to make you job-ready. AI-assisted project feedback, verified certificates, and real portfolio projects set our graduates apart.

### 💼 Mwenaro Talent
We are the bridge between Africa's best tech graduates and the companies that need them. Our Talent Marketplace showcases vetted professionals — software engineers, frontend architects, and product designers — who have completed real production-level projects within the Mwenaro ecosystem.

### 🔬 Mwenaro Labs
Our R&D and software delivery arm. We help startups and enterprises design, build, and maintain scalable web applications, AI automation systems, and digital transformation solutions. We've built internal tools like **Mwenaro Flow** and enterprise solutions like **SafariLink CRM**.
